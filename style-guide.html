A style guide is your trusty companion in the world of design. It's a comprehensive document that outlines the essential elements and guidelines for creating consistent and cohesive visual experiences. From color palettes and typography choices to spacing and layout principles, a style guide serves as a blueprint for maintaining design consistency across various projects. It's like having a design rulebook that ensures everyone on your team speaks the same visual language, resulting in polished and professional-looking designs. With a style guide in hand, you can create harmonious and on-brand designs with ease.
